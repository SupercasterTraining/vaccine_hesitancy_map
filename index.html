Project Overview

This three-part training course was created for Planned Parenthood Pasadena & San Gabriel Valley. It was designed to train volunteers to have productive, effective conversations with vaccine-hesitant individuals with the ultimate goal of increasing people's willingness to get the COVID-19 vaccine. The training is multimedia, contains knowledge checks, and is responsive, meaning it looks just as good on mobile or tablet as it does on a computer.
